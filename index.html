OLD MAN JETT!

Legend has it that once a year OLD MAN JETT can be seen taking off in a plane riding the skies. He's kinda like Cannonball Jenkins from Spongebob but of course a lot older. You can here him exclaim "I knew nothing could come good from these city folks and their accursed Prepaid!". People claim to see a younger version of him around but I highly doubt that is the truth. What I think might be happening is OLD MAN JETT hiring a doppelgänger to do his bidding. That or he's somehow found the fountain of youth. Either way he is old and grumpy. Bah Humbug!
